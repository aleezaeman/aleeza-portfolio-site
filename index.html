My way of getting things done

Fast and transparent, You can have a brand identity that truly represents your business in just 4 weeks.

01.

Understanding the Requirements

In the initial phase, we conduct an interview to thoroughly understand your needs and project requirements. This enables us to create a brand identity that resonates with your unique story and core values.

02.

Research and Strategizing

In the next phase of your brand identity project, I move to dive deep into your brand’s fundamental values and creating a unique story. At the same time, I study your competitors to understand the market landscape and pinpoint strategic ways to stand out.

03.

Design Excecution

In the third phase, we shift our focus to crafting the moodboard, designing logos, establishing a color palette, defining typography, and integrating mockups and layouts. We ensure alignment with the brand’s core values and consider scalability and adaptability for various applications.

04.

Printable and Marketing Materials

In the final phase of your brand identity project, we bring all the brand identity elements together. This includes crafting the moodboard, designing logos, establishing a color palette, defining typography, integrating mockups and layouts, and creating essential marketing materials and printables. These materials—such as brand guidelines, business cards, social media graphics, brochures, posters, and packaging design—align seamlessly with your brand’s core values and visual identity.
